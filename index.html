Bridge at the University of Chicago


About


For over forty years the UChicago Bridge Club has been meeting weekly. We love to play the game of bridge and would like to share it with everyone. We use the 2/1 bidding system. Sometimes we compete in tournaments against other schools. We meet thursdays at 8 in hutch commons (a new location tbd).
