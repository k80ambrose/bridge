Bridge at the University of Chicago

About

For over forty years UChicago's Bridge Club has been meeting weekly to entertain their shared passion for a beautiful and complex card game. Right now we're meeting online but when in-person activities resume we'll be in Ida Noyes Hall on Thursdays at 8. We use 2/1 bidding. To stay up to date with all things Bridge Club join our mailing list
