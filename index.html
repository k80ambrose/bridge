About

For over forty years UChicago's Bridge Club has been meeting weekly to entertain their shared passion for a beautifully complex card game. Whether you're new to bridge or have been playing for years, this community has something to offer. We meet in Ida Noyes on Thursdays at 7:30 and play all night. To stay up to date with all things Bridge Club join our mailing list and follow us on instragram.
