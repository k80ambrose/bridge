Bridge at the University of Chicago

About

For over forty years UChicago's Bridge Club has been meeting weekly to entertain their shared passion for a beautiful and complex card game. We love bridge and want to share it with everyone in the UChicago community who wants to play. Right now we're meeting online but when in-person activities resume we'll be in Ida Noyes Hall [Room #?] on Thursdays at 8. We use 2/1 bidding.
