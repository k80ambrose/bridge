About

For over forty years UChicago's Bridge Club has been meeting weekly to entertain their shared passion for a beautifully complex card game. Whether you're new to bridge or a seasoned player, this community has something special to offer your game. We meet in Ida Noyes on Thursdays nights from 7:30 to 11:00. To stay up to date with all things Bridge Club join our mailing list and follow us on instagram.
