Bridge at the University of Chicago

About


For over forty years UChicago's Bridge Club has been meeting weekly to entertain their shared passion for a wonderful card game. We love bridge and would like to share it with you. Sometimes we compete in tournaments against other schools. We use 2/1 bidding.
